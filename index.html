Weekly Quiz and Mock exams

Our Tutorial offers a comprehensive and flexible learning experience tailored for students preparing for JAMB UTME and WAEC. With regular weekly quizzes to reinforce learning, monthly general tests to evaluate progress, and full-length mock exams that simulate real exam conditions, you'll have all the tools you need to excel.

Our dedicated expert instructors provide continuous support and personalized feedback, ensuring you're always prepared and confident for your assessments. The user-friendly platform makes it easy to monitor your progress, set goals, and stay motivated. Whether you're striving for top scores or seeking to deepen your understanding, our robust assessment system has everything you need to succeed.

Every Friday, we conduct weekly quizzes that cover the material taught during the week. These quizzes help reinforce learning, track progress, and develop exam-taking strategies.

At the end of each month, students participate in a comprehensive test that simulates the JAMB exam format. These tests evaluate understanding, prepare students for the real exam, and reward the top three scorers with a free month of tuition.

As the JAMB exam approaches, we conduct a full-length mock exam to provide students with an authentic exam experience. The top three scorers in this final mock will have their JAMB registration fees sponsored by Brandel Scholars Academy. These mock exams simulate real conditions, provide detailed feedback, and boost performance.
